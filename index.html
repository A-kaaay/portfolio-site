Here's the honest version most people won't tell you: Google's own guidance says there's no secret markup or magic file for this. Showing up in AI answers is mostly the fundamentals done properly, structured so AI systems can lift your answer cleanly and trust the source. I do that, and I tell you plainly what actually moves the needle and what's just hype.
