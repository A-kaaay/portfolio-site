Generative Engine Optimization: making your company visible and cited when buyers ask AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) for recommendations. It overlaps with SEO but has its own rules: how your content is structured, where you're mentioned, and whether AI systems treat your site as a source worth citing.
